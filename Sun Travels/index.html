Our Packages

A glimpse of the splendid sights of the "green land" Chattogram to bring your globetrotting spirit back to life again

Saint Martin Island

Location: Bay of Bengal

Explore the only coral island in Bangladesh, with beaches fringed with coconut palms and laid-back locals

Sajek

Location: Dighinala,Sajek Rd,Sajek

An emerging tourist spot situated among the hills of the Kasalong range, known as the Queen of Hills

Cox's Bazar

Location: Cox's Bazar District

The longest sea beach in the world which is home to bronze statues and centuries-old Buddhist manuscripts

Rangamati

Location: Chittagong Hills region

A beautiful mountainous district & a part of Chittagong hill tracts that serves as a habitation for various tribes.

Bandarban

Location: Bandarban District

The lure of the tallest peaks of Bangladesh, treks through virgin forests and chance to meet more than 15 tribes

Chittagong War Cemetery

Location: 19 Badshah Miah Chowdhury Rd

Created by the British Army,a burial place for the soldiers who died during WWII as a sign of honour
