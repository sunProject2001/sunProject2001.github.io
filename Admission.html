Admission Process

Admission process is similar for both Bachelor's degree and Master's degree.By following the below mentioned steps candidacy can be accomplished effectively for desired programs.


Application period for Bachelor's degree starts from 20th january & ends in 20th March

Application period for Master's degree starts from 10th September & ends in 10th November

STEP 1

Eligibility is the pre-condition for application.To see whether you are elgible or not,Eligibility Criteria is given below :

Eligibility Criteria (B.Sc):

1. You have successfully completed your high school or equivalent with no less than 80% in average.

2. You are not enrolled in any courses in any univeristy.

3. As this is an engineering focused univeristy,you must have to show extraordinary results/extracurricular activites related to Mathematics,Physics or Chemistry.Any kind of contribution towards these fields would be appreciated.

4. Language of instruction in all of the B.Sc Courses are English.So a proof of your proficiency in English is mandatory to enroll in a program.We accept IELTS & TOEFL.

Eligibility Criteria (M.Sc):

1. You have successfully completed your bachelor's degree with cgpa no less than 3.5 .

2. You are not enrolled in any courses in any univeristy.

3. As this is an engineering focused univeristy,you must have to show extraordinary results/extracurricular activites related to Mathematics,Physics or Chemistry.Any kind of contribution towards these fields would be appreciated.

4. Language of instruction in all of the B.Sc Courses are English.So a proof of your proficiency in English is mandatory to enroll in a program.We accept IELTS & TOEFL.

STEP 2

Collect all the documents according to the "Eligibility Criteria" and submit through our online portal.

STEP 3

The Admission committee would evaluate your application,based on which you would be notified whether you are accepted as an candidate for the entrance exam or not.

STEP 4

Complete the Entrance exam and wait for the result.

STEP 5

Based on the result,collect your "Enrollment Pass" and enroll in your desired program

Make sure you got a account on our online portal before beginning with the procedure

Online Potal helps us to keep track of our prospective students for admission purpose and even after enrollment.
