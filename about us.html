Message From Vice-Chancellor

The soul puropose behind the outset of Sun Univeristy back in 1968 was to produce finest minds in order to make the world a easier place for the future generation.It has been serving this motive for the past 53 years to still this day with the highest level of enthusiasm.At present the university has 12 degree rewarding departments with full of the brightest students from around the globe through highly competitive entrance exam.For entire betterment we have already taken some course of action and many initiatives such as taking strategic development plan to set up more departments,directories,research centers etc. Being the Vice-Chancellor of the university i am providing it with all the necessities it's coming across.


Lastly,I am more than happy to welcome the fresh minds into our world of wisdom

Farida Rashid

Vice-Chancellor


Mission & Vision



The only vision we have is to become a self contained technical university that can provide its students with all the necessities and contribute towards making world an easier place for everybody with science and innovation

Being one of the best educational institutions in the world,Our missions change with the course of time.But here are the ones that will always be on our priority list :

  • 1. To improve cooperation among the scholarly world and industry.

  • 2. To Assist with intelligence to the greatest minds so that they can reach to the infinity

    • 3. To Raise proficient and quality engineers.

    4. To make the institution more suitable for the learners.
